Day 1: Heritage, Culture, and Old Ahmedabad

Morning:

8:00 AM - 9:30 AM: Sabarmati Ashram

Start your day with a visit to the Sabarmati Ashram, the residence of Mahatma Gandhi. This peaceful ashram offers museums, exhibits, and tranquil surroundings to explore. The exhibits cover Gandhi's life, philosophy, and his role in India's independence struggle.

9:45 AM - 10:45 AM: Sidi Saiyyed Mosque

A short drive from Sabarmati Ashram, head to the Sidi Saiyyed Mosque. Admire the world-famous Sidi Saiyyed Jali (stone latticework), which is one of the finest examples of Indo-Saracenic architecture.

Mid-Morning:

11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Jama Masjid

Visit Jama Masjid, one of Ahmedabad’s most famous landmarks. The mosque was built in 1424 and is an excellent example of Islamic architecture with intricate carvings and impressive domes.

12:15 PM - 1:00 PM: Bhadra Fort & Teen Darwaza

Next, head to Bhadra Fort, a historical fortification dating back to the 15th century. Explore the fort and its surrounding areas, and don’t miss the Teen Darwaza (Triple Gate), one of the oldest and most famous gates in the city.

Lunch:

1:00 PM - 2:00 PM: Enjoy lunch (you can explore nearby eateries in the old city)

Afternoon:

2:30 PM - 4:00 PM: Calico Museum of Textiles

A visit to the Calico Museum of Textiles is a must if you're interested in India’s rich textile heritage. It's one of the best textile museums in the world, showcasing intricate Indian textiles and rare collections.

Evening:

4:30 PM - 6:00 PM: Kankaria Lake

Head to Kankaria Lake in the evening for a relaxing walk, boat ride, or enjoy recreational activities. The lake is beautiful at sunset, and there are cafes to sit and unwind.

Dinner:

6:30 PM onwards: Relax and enjoy dinner at your convenience.

Day 2: Modern Attractions and Nature

Morning:

8:00 AM - 9:00 AM: Adalaj Stepwell

Begin your day with a trip to Adalaj Stepwell. This 15th-century stepwell, known for its exquisite carvings and architecture, was built as a resting place for travelers. It’s peaceful, historical, and a great place for photos.

9:30 AM - 11:00 AM: Akshardham Temple

Visit the Akshardham Temple, one of the most beautiful temples in India. Admire its stunning architecture and enjoy the exhibits related to Indian culture and spirituality. If time permits, check out the surrounding gardens.

Lunch:

11:30 AM - 1:00 PM: Lunch (You can pick a local spot on your way to the next destination)

Afternoon:

1:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Shreyas Folk Museum

Take a trip to Shreyas Folk Museum, where you can explore the rich cultural history of Gujarat through folk art and exhibits that showcase traditional crafts and rural life.

3:15 PM - 4:30 PM: Science City

Head to Science City for a fun and informative experience. This large complex features exhibits on various scientific fields, including an IMAX theatre, a planetarium, and interactive displays. It’s a perfect stop for families or science enthusiasts.

Evening:

5:00 PM - 6:00 PM: Vastrapur Lake

After exploring Science City, relax at Vastrapur Lake. It’s a peaceful place for an evening stroll or a boat ride. It’s quieter compared to Kankaria Lake and perfect for unwinding.

6:30 PM - 7:30 PM: Law Garden

Conclude your trip with a visit to Law Garden, which is a popular green space near the Law College. You’ll find a vibrant local atmosphere with local handicrafts and traditional goods for sale.

Dinner:

8:00 PM onwards: Enjoy a relaxing dinner and reflect on your trip.
